Personal Statement

I am a dedicated researcher specializing in transcriptional regulation and tumor immune evasion. My work spans two complementary areas: leveraging multi-omics data to uncover transcriptional regulatory patterns and identify novel mechanisms and clinical targets, and applying machine learning to define tumor subtypes and discover therapeutic targets in immunity. These efforts converge on exploring how transcriptional regulation drives immune evasion, advancing cancer biology research and enabling innovations in precision medicine.
